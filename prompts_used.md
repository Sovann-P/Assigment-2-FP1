## Part 2

### Prompt 1
Research four commercial CRM systems, pick any four out of these: Salesforce, HubSpot CRM, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM, and Oracle CRM. After researching them, create a comparison table with these: product, target customer, strengths, weaknesses, and pricing model.

### Prompt 2
Research 3 open source CRM systems, pick any 3 out of these: SuiteCRM, EspoCRM, Odoo CRM, Vtiger CRM. After researching them, create a comparison table with these: product, features, technology stack, community support, and ease of installation.

## AI Effectiveness Analysis

For this research, AI did a good job in helping to research the various topics. Making comparisons as well as giving summaries of the research was extremely helpful and time efficient. I also asked it to site its references so that I knew where to look to validate the information that it was giving me. One area where AI struggled was when asking it questions about setting up one of the CRM platforms. It did explain how to create the account very well and left out important information such as needing a company domain and website in order to use some of them. What I would do differently next time is to be more specific in prompting so that no missing information is left out. I would definitely trust AI for researching, however I need to specifically prompt it to cite its references in order to fully trust it instead of just asking the questions.
